Hi! My name is Mehmet, and I am a second-year M.S. student under the supervision of Prof. Ergün Yalçın in department of mathematics at Bilkent University. I also received my Bachelor's degree from the Mathematics department of Bilkent universiy in 2022.

My research interest lies in algebraic topology: Homotopy, homology, cohomology, and category theory. Currently, I am studying the homotopy theory of simplicial objects and the cohomology of small categories as a part of my master's thesis. My CV is embedded in this website as well.
